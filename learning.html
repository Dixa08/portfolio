PES Lab Learning Journey

Lab 1: Introduction to Personality Enhancement Skills

In the first lab, we were introduced to the importance of personality development and communication skills. Ma'am guided us on how to improve our English speaking ability and build confidence while communicating.

We also learned how to create a professional LinkedIn profile and how to present ourselves online. The session included useful tips for speaking good and clear English in daily life and professional environments.

Key Learning: Confidence in communication, professional online presence, and English improvement techniques.

Lab 2: Self Analysis and Interview Preparation

In this lab, we worked on self-analysis to understand our strengths, weaknesses, interests, and personality traits. We practiced answering common interview questions and learned how to respond confidently.

We were also taught how to introduce ourselves properly in interviews, including structuring our introduction in a clear and professional way.

Key Learning: Self awareness, interview confidence, and structured self introduction.

Lab 3: Presentation Skills and Professional Behaviour

This lab focused on developing presentation skills. We learned how to prepare, organize, and deliver an effective presentation. We were taught about body language, voice modulation, and audience engagement.

We also discussed professional skills and how to present ourselves properly in academic and professional environments.

Key Learning: Public speaking confidence, presentation structure, and professional communication skills.

Lab 4: Group Presentation and Team Collaboration

In this session, we performed group presentations. This activity helped us experience teamwork, coordination, and collaborative learning.

We learned how to divide responsibilities, support team members, and present ideas together effectively. It improved our communication, leadership, and confidence while speaking in front of others.

Key Learning: Teamwork, collaboration, confidence, and real presentation experience.
